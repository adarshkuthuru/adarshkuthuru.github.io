Job similarity with seniority matching

In this project, we employ Natural Language Processing (NLP) to give job recommendations to candidates based on their previous applications. We perform two rounds of matching to identify if two jobs are related based on the job description text. The first is similarity macthing based on cosine similarities of the sentence embeddings generated using a variant of SBERT transformer model. In the second round, we identify seniority related text in the job descriptions, train the model by assigning seniority labels to each sentence, and then identify whether two jobs are of same seniority level based on the cosine similarities of the seniority sentences.
